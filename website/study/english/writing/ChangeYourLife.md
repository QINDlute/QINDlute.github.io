# Change Your Life

<text-reader>
Hello, Today I want to talk to you about something important. I want to talk about your life. Yes, your life. You can change it.
</text-reader>

<text-reader>
Sometimes life is hard. We feel tired. We feel sad. Maybe we don't like our job. Maybe we feel lost. But this is not the end. You are still here. You still have time. You can start again.

You do not need to be perfect. You do not need to be rich. You do not need to know everything. You only need to say, "I will try." Small things can make a big change. Wake up early. Drink water. Be kind. Read one page. Take a short walk. Say "thank you."
</text-reader>

<text-reader>
Do one good thing today. Do another tomorrow. After one week, you may feel better. You will make mistakes. You will feel tired again. That is okay. This is part of life.

Do not stop. Do not give up. Say to yourself, "I can do this. My life is important." Even if it is hard, say it every day. One day, you will believe it.
</text-reader>

<text-reader>
No one can live your life for you. You have power. You have a choice.

So take a deep breath. Stand up. And say, "Today is the first day of my new life." You are not too late. You are not too old. You are not too weak. You are strong. You are ready. Yes, you can change your life.
</text-reader>
